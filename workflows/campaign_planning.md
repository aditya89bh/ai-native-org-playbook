# Campaign Planning Workflow

## Current workflow

Campaign planning coordinates goals, audience, positioning, content, channels, budget, review, launch, and reporting.

## AI-native redesign

Agents support audience research, brief drafting, content variant planning, review checklists, experiment tracking, and performance summaries.

## Human steps

- Define strategy
- Approve positioning
- Own brand judgment
- Approve public claims
- Allocate budget

## Agent steps

- Draft campaign brief
- Summarize audience research
- Generate content checklist
- Track experiments
- Prepare reporting notes

## Shared steps

- Creative review
- Launch checklist
- Performance review
- Experiment retrospective

## Memory events

- Campaign brief saved
- Positioning decision logged
- Experiment recorded
- Performance summary captured
- Lesson reused

## Governance points

Humans approve public messaging, brand claims, pricing claims, customer references, and regulated topics.

## Metrics

- Campaign cycle time
- Content throughput
- Experiment reuse
- Message consistency
- Lead quality

## Risks

- Generic content
- Unsupported claims
- Off-brand messaging
- Weak experiment discipline
