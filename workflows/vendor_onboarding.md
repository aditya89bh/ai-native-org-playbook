# Vendor Onboarding Workflow

## Current workflow

Vendor onboarding coordinates intake, qualification, risk review, procurement, finance setup, contract review, and operational handoff.

## AI-native redesign

Agents support vendor intake, checklist tracking, policy retrieval, risk flagging, document comparison, and approval routing.

## Human steps

- Approve vendor selection
- Negotiate terms
- Approve financial commitment
- Own risk exceptions
- Manage relationship-sensitive issues

## Agent steps

- Prepare vendor packet
- Track onboarding checklist
- Retrieve policies
- Flag missing documents
- Draft handoff summary

## Shared steps

- Vendor review
- Contract review
- Finance setup
- Operational handoff

## Memory events

- Vendor profile created
- Risk review recorded
- Contract status logged
- Approval captured
- Handoff memory saved

## Governance points

Humans approve vendor selection, contract terms, payment setup, security exceptions, and risk waivers.

## Metrics

- Onboarding cycle time
- Checklist completion
- Approval latency
- Vendor risk visibility
- Handoff quality

## Risks

- Missing approvals
- Weak risk review
- Poor contract traceability
- Vendor context loss
