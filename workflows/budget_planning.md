# Budget Planning Workflow

## Current workflow

Budget planning collects assumptions, forecasts, targets, tradeoffs, approvals, and revisions across teams.

## AI-native redesign

Agents support assumption retrieval, scenario drafting, variance context, budget packet preparation, and approval tracking while humans own allocation decisions.

## Human steps

- Set strategic priorities
- Approve allocations
- Resolve tradeoffs
- Own financial risk
- Communicate final budget

## Agent steps

- Retrieve prior assumptions
- Draft budget scenarios
- Flag missing inputs
- Summarize variance history
- Track approvals

## Shared steps

- Scenario review
- Department budget review
- Variance explanation
- Forecast revision

## Memory events

- Budget assumption logged
- Scenario rationale recorded
- Approval captured
- Revision reason saved
- Variance note updated

## Governance points

Humans approve budgets, forecasts, payment commitments, investor-facing numbers, and material changes.

## Metrics

- Planning cycle time
- Forecast accuracy
- Approval latency
- Assumption traceability
- Budget variance quality

## Risks

- Wrong assumptions
- Weak audit trail
- Overconfident forecasts
- Unauthorized financial commitment
