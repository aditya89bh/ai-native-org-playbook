# Product Discovery Workflow

## Current workflow

Product discovery turns customer needs, market signals, constraints, and business goals into validated product opportunities.

## AI-native redesign

Agents cluster feedback, retrieve prior decisions, compare evidence, draft discovery briefs, and maintain hypothesis memory while humans own insight and prioritization.

## Human steps

- Define discovery goals
- Conduct high-context interviews
- Interpret evidence
- Prioritize opportunities
- Approve product direction

## Agent steps

- Cluster feedback
- Summarize interviews
- Retrieve related roadmap memory
- Draft opportunity briefs
- Flag weak evidence

## Shared steps

- Hypothesis definition
- Evidence review
- Opportunity sizing
- Experiment planning

## Memory events

- Hypothesis recorded
- Evidence linked
- Customer signal captured
- Decision rationale logged
- Experiment outcome saved

## Governance points

Humans approve roadmap changes, customer commitments, pricing implications, and public product claims.

## Metrics

- Discovery cycle time
- Evidence quality
- Decision reuse
- Experiment learning rate
- Roadmap churn

## Risks

- Treating summaries as insight
- Over-weighting loud feedback
- Weak evidence traceability
- Roadmap drift
