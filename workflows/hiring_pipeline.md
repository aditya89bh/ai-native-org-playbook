# Hiring Pipeline Workflow

## Current workflow

Hiring usually moves through intake, sourcing, screening, interviews, feedback, decision, offer, and onboarding handoff.

## AI-native redesign

Agents support coordination, packet preparation, rubric checks, scheduling, feedback synthesis, and missing-signal alerts while humans keep hiring judgment.

## Human steps

- Define role requirements
- Evaluate candidate fit
- Conduct interviews
- Make final hiring decision
- Approve offer and compensation

## Agent steps

- Prepare candidate packets
- Track stage progress
- Summarize interview notes
- Check rubric completeness
- Draft coordination messages

## Shared steps

- Interview planning
- Feedback review
- Hiring debrief
- Onboarding handoff

## Memory events

- Role requirement recorded
- Candidate stage updated
- Interview feedback summarized
- Hiring decision logged
- Onboarding handoff created

## Governance points

Humans approve candidate advancement, rejection, compensation, and final hiring decisions.

## Metrics

- Time to screen
- Time to schedule
- Packet completeness
- Candidate experience
- Rubric adherence

## Risks

- Bias amplification
- Privacy leakage
- Over-reliance on incomplete evidence
- Poor candidate communication
