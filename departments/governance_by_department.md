# Governance by Department

## Low to medium governance departments

These departments can usually begin with assistive agents and bounded execution:

- Marketing
- Sales
- Operations
- Customer support
- Research
- Design
- Product

## High governance departments

These departments need stronger controls from the start:

- Engineering
- HR
- Finance
- Legal and compliance

## Governance controls

| Control | Medium governance | High governance |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Approval | Sampled or threshold-based | Pre-approval for consequential actions |
| Audit | Action logs | Full source, input, output, approval logs |
| Memory | Scoped workflow memory | Strict retention and access rules |
| Metrics | Quality and speed | Quality, risk, compliance, and correction |
| Escalation | Confidence and risk triggers | Policy, legal, financial, and sensitive-data triggers |
