# Department Redesign Workshop Agenda

## Duration

2 to 3 hours

## Participants

- Department head
- Workflow owners
- Tool owners
- Governance or compliance representative
- AI transformation lead

## Section 1: Current state

- What work does the department own?
- What workflows consume the most time?
- Where do handoffs break?
- What knowledge gets lost?

## Section 2: Agent opportunities

- Which work is repetitive?
- Which work is context-heavy?
- Which work needs review?
- Which work should stay human-led?

## Section 3: Memory design

- What should be remembered?
- What should expire?
- Who can correct memory?
- What should never be stored?

## Section 4: Governance

- What requires approval?
- What can agents execute?
- What triggers escalation?
- What needs audit logs?

## Section 5: Pilot selection

- Pick one bounded workflow
- Define baseline metrics
- Assign human owner
- Define review cadence
