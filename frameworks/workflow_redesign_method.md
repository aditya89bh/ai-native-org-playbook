# Workflow Redesign Method

Use this method before assigning agents to work.

## Step 1: Map the current workflow

Capture steps, owners, tools, delays, decisions, handoffs, and failure points.

## Step 2: Classify each step

Classify every step as observe, decide, act, review, remember, or escalate.

## Step 3: Identify agent opportunities

Score repetition, context load, decision frequency, data quality, risk, and human judgment required.

## Step 4: Redesign the workflow

Assign each step to human-led, agent-assisted, agent-executed, or shared ownership.

## Step 5: Add memory

Define what events must be captured, retrieved, corrected, and forgotten.

## Step 6: Add governance

Define permissions, approval thresholds, escalation triggers, and audit logs.

## Step 7: Measure before and after

Track cycle time, quality, risk, cost, human load, and knowledge reuse.
