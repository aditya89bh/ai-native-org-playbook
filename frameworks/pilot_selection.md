# Pilot Selection Framework

Choose pilots that prove value without exposing the organization to unnecessary risk.

## Good pilot traits

- Clear workflow boundary
- Known baseline metrics
- Repetitive work
- Accessible data
- Low reversibility cost
- Engaged human owner
- Visible business value

## Bad pilot traits

- Unclear owner
- Poor documentation
- High legal exposure
- Sensitive relationships
- No measurement baseline
- Political resistance

## Pilot scorecard

Score every candidate from 1 to 5 on value, feasibility, risk, measurability, and sponsor quality.

Start with the highest-value pilot that is still easy to govern.
