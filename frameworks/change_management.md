# Change Management for AI-Native Organizations

AI-native transformation changes how people work, decide, review, and trust systems.

## Change risks

- Employees feel replaced instead of supported
- Managers lose visibility into work
- Agents create hidden quality issues
- Teams use tools without shared standards
- Governance arrives too late

## Change principles

1. Start with pain, not technology.
2. Keep humans accountable for consequential decisions.
3. Show before-and-after workflow evidence.
4. Train people on supervision, not only prompting.
5. Reward knowledge reuse and process improvement.

## Adoption sequence

Assist, review, govern, orchestrate, then redesign.
