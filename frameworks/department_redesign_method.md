# Department Redesign Method

Department redesign turns a traditional function into a human-agent operating unit.

## Step 1: Identify department jobs

List recurring jobs, decision loops, reporting needs, customer touchpoints, and compliance duties.

## Step 2: Separate work types

Classify work into judgment, coordination, research, production, review, administration, and memory.

## Step 3: Define agent roles

Create agent roles around durable responsibilities, not one-off prompts.

## Step 4: Redesign reporting lines

Agents do not replace org charts. They create parallel operating lines for information, review, and execution.

## Step 5: Define new human roles

AI-native departments need owners for agent quality, memory hygiene, workflow design, and governance.

## Step 6: Define KPIs

Measure speed, quality, risk, cost, learning, and human load.
