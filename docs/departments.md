# Department Playbooks

The department playbooks translate the AI-native organization framework into specific business functions.

## Included departments

- Sales
- Marketing
- Product
- Engineering
- Design
- HR
- Finance
- Operations
- Customer Support
- Research
- Legal and Compliance

## How to use department playbooks

1. Pick one department.
2. Read the current operating model and AI-native redesign.
3. Identify human-led, agent-assisted, and agent-executed work.
4. Select one pilot workflow.
5. Define agent roles, memory, governance, and metrics.
6. Use the department JSON spec to score redesign complexity.

## CLI example

```bash
ai-org-playbook department departments/specs/sales.json
```

## Design rule

Do not redesign every department at once. Choose one department with a clear workflow, visible business value, and manageable governance risk.
