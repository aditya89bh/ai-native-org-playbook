# Example: Sales Research Agent Run

## Workflow

Discovery call preparation.

## Input

- Target account
- CRM notes
- Prior calls
- Sales playbook
- Public company context

## Agent actions

1. Retrieve account memory.
2. Summarize stakeholder context.
3. Identify likely business pains.
4. Draft discovery questions.
5. Flag unsupported assumptions.

## Output

- Account brief
- Discovery question list
- Risk notes
- Follow up context

## Human review

Account executive reviews the brief, corrects wrong context, and approves customer-facing follow up.

## Success metric

Reduced call preparation time with higher account-context quality.
