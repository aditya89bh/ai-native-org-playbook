# Example: Chief of Staff Agent Run

## Workflow

Weekly leadership operating review.

## Input

- Project updates
- Meeting notes
- KPI snapshot
- Decision log
- Strategic priorities

## Agent actions

1. Summarize major changes.
2. Surface blocked priorities.
3. Retrieve related decisions.
4. Draft leadership brief.
5. Create follow-up tracker.

## Output

- Weekly leadership brief
- Decision summary
- Risk notes
- Follow-up tracker

## Human review

Founder, chief of staff, or operating leader approves priorities, commitments, and sensitive notes.

## Success metric

Better leadership visibility with fewer missed decisions and follow-ups.
