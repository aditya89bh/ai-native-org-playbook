# Example: Governance Review for Vendor Onboarding

## Action

Approve a new vendor contract.

## Risk profile

- Financial impact: high
- Legal exposure: high
- Reversibility: low
- Public visibility: low

## Recommended approval

Pre-approval or dual approval depending on contract value.

## Required audit

- Vendor packet
- Contract version
- Risk review
- Approval owner
- Decision rationale
- Payment setup status

## Human owner

Operations lead with finance and legal review.
